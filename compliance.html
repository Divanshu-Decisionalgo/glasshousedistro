Compliance & Regulatory

Glass House Distro operates with full regulatory transparency. This page outlines our compliance policies, age verification requirements, and legal obligations for wholesale partners.

verified_user

21+ Wholesale Platform

Glass House Distro is a business-to-business platform exclusively for adults 21 years of age or older. Access to our pricing, catalog, and ordering system requires age verification and a valid wholesale business account. We do not sell to consumers or minors under any circumstances.

1. Age Verification Policy

All users of the Glass House Distro platform must be 21 years of age or older. We employ the following age verification measures:

  • Account registration requires confirmation of age (21+) via checkbox attestation
  • Business accounts are reviewed manually; accounts appearing to be registered by minors will be immediately terminated
  • Wholesale partners are contractually required to enforce age restrictions at point-of-sale in their own retail environments
  • We reserve the right to request government-issued ID verification at any time

2. Business-to-Business Restriction

Glass House Distro operates exclusively as a wholesale distributor. Products sold through our platform are intended for resale to adult consumers by licensed retail businesses. We strictly prohibit:

  • Direct consumer sales by Glass House Distro to the general public
  • Purchase of products for personal use through wholesale accounts
  • Transfer of wholesale accounts to unauthorized third parties
  • Resale of products purchased at wholesale prices to other distributors (without written authorization)

3. Business License Requirement

All wholesale partners must hold and maintain in good standing:

  • A valid business license or certificate of incorporation in their operating state/jurisdiction
  • Any applicable retail tobacco, smoke shop, or specialty retail license required by state law
  • A valid reseller's permit or sales tax exemption certificate where required

Wholesale partners agree to provide copies of required licenses upon request and to notify us immediately if any license lapses, is suspended, or is revoked. Accounts in non-compliance may be suspended without prior notice.

4. Product Compliance

All products sold by Glass House Distro are marketed and sold strictly as tobacco products, novelty items, or legal consumer goods as applicable to each product category. Wholesale partners agree to:

  • Market and sell products only in accordance with applicable federal, state, and local laws
  • Not make claims about products that are not substantiated or that violate advertising regulations
  • Comply with all FDA, FTC, and applicable state regulations governing the sale of tobacco accessories
  • Properly age-gate the sale of all products to consumers in their retail environment

5. State-Level Compliance

Regulations governing the sale of tobacco accessories, glass products, and related items vary by state and municipality. Wholesale partners are solely responsible for ensuring compliance with:

  • State and local licensing requirements
  • Point-of-sale age verification laws
  • Signage and disclosure requirements
  • Any jurisdiction-specific bans or restrictions on specific product types

Glass House Distro does not provide legal compliance advice. We recommend consulting with a licensed attorney familiar with tobacco retail law in your jurisdiction.

6. Reporting Violations

If you believe a Glass House Distro wholesale partner is selling products illegally, to minors, or in violation of our compliance standards, please report it to us at glasshousedistributors@gmail.com. All reports are taken seriously and investigated promptly.

7. Record Keeping

Glass House Distro maintains transaction records as required by applicable law. Wholesale partners are encouraged to maintain their own records of purchases for tax and regulatory compliance purposes. We provide detailed invoices and order histories accessible through your account dashboard.

8. Contact for Compliance Questions

For compliance-related inquiries, contact our team at glasshousedistributors@gmail.com or submit a request through our contact page.

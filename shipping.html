Glass House Distro is committed to delivering your wholesale orders safely and on time. This policy outlines our shipping procedures, timelines, and carrier options.

1. Order Processing Time

Orders are processed on business days (Monday–Friday, excluding federal holidays). After payment is confirmed:

  • Standard Orders: Processed within 1–3 business days
  • Large/Freight Orders (100+ units): Processed within 3–5 business days
  • Custom/Specialty Orders: Processing timeline provided at time of quote

Orders placed after 2:00 PM EST will be processed the following business day.

2. Shipping Methods & Estimated Delivery

We ship via the following carriers based on order size and destination:

  • UPS Ground: 3–7 business days (standard domestic)
  • FedEx 2-Day: 2 business days (expedited domestic)
  • FedEx Overnight: Next business day (urgent orders; additional fee applies)
  • Freight (LTL/FTL): 5–10 business days (large pallet orders)

Delivery estimates are based on carrier projections and do not include processing time. Glass House Distro is not responsible for carrier delays outside our control.

3. Packaging Standards

All glass products are shipped using our proprietary packaging system:

  • Custom foam-molded inserts for each product SKU to prevent movement and breakage
  • Double-wall corrugated outer boxes rated for drop and impact protection
  • Void fill material for mixed-SKU orders with varying sizes
  • Discreet outer packaging — no brand markings visible on exterior

Our packaging has achieved a <0.5% breakage rate across all orders. In the rare event of transit damage, see our Damaged Items policy below.

4. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on:

  • Order weight and dimensional weight
  • Destination zip code
  • Selected shipping method

Free shipping is available on qualifying orders over $500 (standard ground shipping to contiguous US states only). This threshold may be adjusted during promotional periods.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email containing:

  • Tracking number(s) and carrier information
  • Estimated delivery date
  • Link to track your shipment in real time

For orders with multiple boxes or pallets, you will receive a tracking number for each shipment.

6. International Shipping

We currently ship to Canada and select international destinations on a case-by-case basis. International orders are subject to:

  • Additional shipping costs calculated at time of order
  • Customs duties and import taxes (buyer's responsibility)
  • Compliance with destination country's import regulations
  • Longer processing and transit times (10–21 business days typical)

Contact us before placing an international order to confirm eligibility and shipping costs.

7. Damaged or Lost Items

If your order arrives damaged or is lost in transit:

  • For damaged items: Photograph the damage and original packaging, then contact us within 5 business days of delivery at glasshousedistributors@gmail.com
  • For lost packages: Contact us after the carrier's estimated delivery date has passed. We will file a claim on your behalf and arrange a replacement or credit
  • Do not discard damaged products or packaging until your claim is resolved

All shipments are fully insured. Approved claims are resolved within 5–10 business days.

8. Address Changes & Refusals

Address changes must be requested before the order ships. Once a shipment is in transit, address changes may incur carrier fees which will be passed on to the customer. Refused deliveries are subject to a restocking fee of up to 20% of the order value plus return shipping costs.

9. Questions

For shipping inquiries, contact us at glasshousedistributors@gmail.com or submit a request. Please include your order number when reaching out.
